  • JANUARY 9-12, 2019

    Vancouver, BC

    Part of the Canadian University Press’s 80-plus-year tradition, NASH82 will be hosted by The Ubyssey — UBC’s independent student publication — at the Vancouver Marriott Pinnacle Downtown Hotel.

With the theme of “Empower,” this year’s conference reaffirms our belief that student media is media.
We will offer workshops, panels and roundtables that amplify student journalists’ ability and responsibility to report on difficult and emerging issues — sexual violence, hate speech, the environment, poverty, diversity and more — with care. We will also highlight how student journalists can empower themselves to be heard in professional spaces and how student papers can navigate systemic challenges.
We look forward to supporting your growth and having you join The Ubyssey in our shenanigans!
