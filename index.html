About Us

NASH82 is a conference for journalism community leaders! Here's your chance to meet our friends from Publisher and join community organizers just like you.

What is NASH82

NASH82 is the official conference for journalism community leaders. It's an opportunity to meet other communities, learn from one another, and tackle the issues that we face together. With a full schedule of workshops, talks, and discussion sessions, organizers can learn best practices for running a publication, share their experiences, and help guide the next generation.
