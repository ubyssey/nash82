ABOUT US - What is NASH82?

The Ubyssey is excited to host NASH 82 in Vancouver from January 9-12, 2019.

With the theme of “Empower,” this year’s conference reaffirms our belief that student media is media. We will offer workshops, panels and roundtable discussions that amplify student journalists’ ability and responsibility to report on difficult and emerging issues — sexual violence, hate speech, the environment, poverty, diversity and more — with care. We will also highlight how student journalists can empower themselves to be heard in professional spaces, as well as how student papers can navigate change and challenges to their structure. We look forward to supporting your growth, hearing your opinions and having you join The Ubyssey in our shenanigans!
